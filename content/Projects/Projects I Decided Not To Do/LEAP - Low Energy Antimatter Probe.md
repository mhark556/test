LEAP (Low Energy Antimatter Probe) is a [[Projects I Decided Not To Do|project I decided not to do]] for a variety of reasons. After extensive research on scintillation tubes, silicon photomultipliers and lead canisters, I decided that constructing LEAP would be impractical because the sensitivity needed to detect antimatter was too great with my budget constraints. Despite being a fascinating idea, I had to concede that with the resources available to me, I couldn't build LEAP.
